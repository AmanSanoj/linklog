What is LinkLog?

LinkLog is a powerful URL shortener and analytics platform that provides detailed insights about link interactions. Track over 40+ data points per visitor with our advanced detection system.

Unlike other alternatives such as Grabify, LinkLog offers comprehensive analytics while maintaining user privacy and providing transparent data collection practices.

Is this legal to use?
Yes, LinkLog operates within legal boundaries. We collect publicly available information that websites normally access. However, using this data to harm or doxx others is prohibited.
What if someone got my IP address?
IP addresses are not private information - every website you visit can see your IP. LinkLog simply makes this visible to link creators. Your physical address cannot be determined from IP alone.
Is LinkLog free?
Yes, LinkLog is completely free and ad-free. We're self-hosted with minimal operating costs, allowing us to provide this service without monetization.
How do I delete my data?
You can request data deletion by visiting our legal page where you'll find instructions for data removal.
