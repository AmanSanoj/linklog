What is LinkLog?

LinkLog is a powerful URL shortener and tracker and Grabify alternative that provides detailed insights about link interactions. Track hundreds of data points per visitor with our advanced fingerprinting.

Unlike Grabify, LinkLog isn't sketchy, is significantly more transparent, powerful, and easy to use.

Is this legal to use?
Yep! We collect publicly available information that every website you go to can access. However, using this data to harm or doxx others is illegal.
What if someone got my IP address?
IP addresses are not private information ‚Äî every website you visit can see your IP. LinkLog simply makes this visible to link creators. Your physical address cannot be determined from IP alone.
Is LinkLog free?
Yes, LinkLog is completely free and ad-free. It's self-hosted with minimal operating costs so I don't really care about monetizing this.
